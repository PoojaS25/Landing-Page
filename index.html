What is Cameo?

Cameo is an innovative camera app for both of ios and Android that lets you capture the best moments in life either on still photos and video. Cameo have many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editing tools,filters and effects.And when you're done you can easily share your result with all of your friends and rest of the world through Instagram.

Cameo has been designed from the bottom up by photography experts and award-winning app developers to make sure you get the best camera app ever created.We are continuosly improving the app with new features-this is just the beginning.
